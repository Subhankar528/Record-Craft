# Record Craft - The Face of Lab Record (OUTR)  

**Record Craft** is a web application designed for **OUTR (Odisha University of Technology and Research)** students to generate professional lab record front pages effortlessly. Built with React, it allows users to input their details and download a well-formatted PDF of their lab record cover page.  

## Features  
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple form to enter all necessary details.  
- **PDF Generation**: Instantly download a properly formatted front page for your lab records.  
- **Responsive Design**: Works on both desktop and mobile devices.  
- **Customizable**: Supports different lab subjects and semester details.

## How to Use  
1. Visit [Record Craft](https://recordcraft.netlify.app/).  
2. Fill in your details (Name, Regd No, Branch, Semester, Subject, etc.).  
3. Click **"Generate PDF"** to download your lab record front page.
